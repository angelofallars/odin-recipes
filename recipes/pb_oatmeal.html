PB/Banana Oatmeal

Healthy, Filling, and Delicious

Peanut butter and bananas are known to be a delicious combo. That's why in this recipe, we'll make use of both of them to make a sweet and hearty oatmeal bowl.

Oatmeal is very filling and is a great way to start your day. This recipe is easy and only takes about 10 minutes to make!

Ingredients

  • Rolled oats
  • Bananas, sliced
  • Peanut butter
  • Cinnamon
  • Salt

Steps

  1. In a boiling pot or pan, add oats, cinnamon, and salt. Cook on medium-high heat for about 7 to 9 minutes and stir well.
  2. Add the banana slices about 2/3rds the way into cooking the oats so that they'll retain their solid and chunky consistency.
  3. When the oats are finished cooking, add the peanut butter, stir them and serve.

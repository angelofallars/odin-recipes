Fried Eggs (Very Fried)

Crispy, Golden Goodness

Let's face it. Although crispy, browned eggs don't always look aesthetically perfect, we all know that they're the real deal.

In this recipe, we'll be cooking sunny-side-up eggs in almost-boiling butter to quickly fry eggs with a good crunch.

Ingredients

  • 2-4 fresh eggs
  • 2 tsp. butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Heat the butter in a nonstick skillet over medium-high heat until very hot.
  2. Crack the eggs and add them to the pan right away. With a cooking spoon, continuously pour oil on top of the egg whites to cook the top well.
  3. Cook in medium-high heat until the bottom part of the whites is brown and crispy. Season with salt and pepper and serve immediately.
